National Park Cycle Tour

A map of a fictitious bike race starting near Salt Lake City, UT and ending in Vancouver, BC. The finished terrain map was acheived through the processing of Digital Elevation Model (DEM) and land cover layers in ArcMap, with final styling applied in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

Oil in the Pigeon River Country

An infographic telling the history and impact of the Michigan Natural Resources Trust Fund. Preprocessing of the maps was done in ArcMap with styling finalized in Adobe Illustrator. The infographic was compiled and finished in Illustrator.

Wild and Open Spaces in an Urban Era

This map combines 2010 Urban Area data (U.S Census Bureau) and USA Parks data (Esri, TomTom) to visualize parks in and around the 45 largest urban areas in the U.S. With a glance the reader can see which cities have the highest number of park acres per capita versus parks acres within 200 miles. ArcMap was used to gather and process the data. Adobe Illustrator was used for the final styling of the map.

2014 Pollutants Released to Land

This web map shows data from the Canadian National Pollutant Release Inventory for 2014. Data points are colored per industry and interactive pop-ups show additional information including the company, industry, and tons released. This map was built with LeafletJS.
